About

Sleep (or lack thereof)

Due to the recent COVID-19 outbreak, I'm sure everyone's sleep schedule is affected. However, my friend's sleep schedule was turned upside down - quite literally. He ended up sleeping in the morning and waking up late in the evening, as if he was suffering jetlag. After googling a few timezones, I found out that his sleep schedule matches that of people on the other side of the world. I wanted to automate this process and let everyone discover where their actual timezone is. Hence, the Sleep Zone tool was born.
